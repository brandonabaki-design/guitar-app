How to play

Left hand (fretboard): Press anywhere on a string to fret that note. Pressing closer to the body raises the pitch. Multi-touch — you can hold several frets at once.

Right hand (strum bar): Tap a string to pluck just that one. Swipe across the bar to strum a chord. Drag down for a downstroke, up for an upstroke.

Chord shortcuts: Tap a chord name (G, C, D…) to auto-fret a chord shape, then strum freely. Tap — to release.

  • Sound is generated live with Karplus-Strong synthesis — no samples.
  • Designed for landscape tablets; multi-touch is the whole point.
  • String tuning is standard E A D G B E.
