How to play

Hold the tablet like a guitar. Fretboard is on the right, strum bar on the left — the same hand positions you'd use on a real instrument.

Fretting hand: Press anywhere on a string to fret that note. Pressing on a higher fret raises the pitch. Multi-touch — hold several frets at once.

Strumming hand: Tap a string on the strum bar to pluck just that one. Swipe across to strum a chord — drag direction controls down vs. up stroke, speed controls velocity.

Chord shortcuts: Tap G, C, D… to auto-fret a chord, then strum freely. Tap — to release.

Fullscreen: Tap the ⛶ icon to hide system UI and suppress edge-swipe gestures while you play.

  • Sound is generated live with Karplus-Strong synthesis — no samples.
  • Designed for landscape tablets; multi-touch is the whole point.
  • Standard tuning: E A D G B e.
